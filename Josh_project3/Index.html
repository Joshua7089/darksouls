So you decided to tackle Dark Souls?

Baby steps

The key to Dark Souls is understanding. You must be ready to die and to learn from your mistakes. Taking it slow and finding each enemies weakness and how to fight them is the key to survivng. The best bit of advice one can recieve is to never take on more than you know you can take on. This website is designed to give you a knowing first step into the dark souls world.
